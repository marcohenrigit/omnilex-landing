02 The Mind & the platform

The work is done by the Mind. The judgment stays with our lawyers.

The Omnilex Mind carries out legal work at a scale and speed no team could match by hand. Our lawyers supervise it through the Mind platform — so every matter carries the accountability of a law firm.

The Mind — does the work

An operating system for legal work

The Mind reasons over primary law, drafts and cites, and runs each matter through intake, research, analysis and delivery. It works on many matters at once, consistently, and shows its sources for everything it produces.

Omnilex counsel — supervises

Qualified lawyers over the platform

Through the Mind platform, our lawyers set the approach for each matter, review the Mind's reasoning, and approve what goes out. Nothing reaches the client unsupervised — you get the throughput of the Mind with a lawyer's accountability.
