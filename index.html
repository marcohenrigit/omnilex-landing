01 The problem

General-purpose AI cannot be trusted for legal work. It paraphrases, it invents citations, and it cannot show you the law it relied on.

An answer you can't trace is an answer you can't use.

In legal practice, the reasoning matters as much as the conclusion. A tool that cannot point to the statute, the decision, or the commentary behind its answer leaves you to redo the work by hand.

Omnilex is built the other way around.

The source comes first. Every answer is assembled from primary law and returned with its citations intact — exactly as you saw above. Verification is a matter of following a link, not repeating the research.

02 How it works

From a real legal question to a cited, verifiable answer

A workflow rebuilt around AI — not a chatbot placed on top of legal research. It is the same sequence the console runs above.

01

Ask a substantive question

Pose it as you would to a senior associate — in plain language, with the full context of the matter. Set the jurisdiction and sources in scope.

02
