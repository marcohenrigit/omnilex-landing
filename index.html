01 The problem

General-purpose AI cannot be trusted for legal work. It paraphrases, it invents citations, and it cannot show you the law it relied on.

An answer you can't trace is an answer you can't use.

In legal practice, the reasoning matters as much as the conclusion. A tool that cannot point to the statute, the decision, or the commentary behind its answer leaves you to redo the work by hand.

Omnilex is built the other way around.

The source comes first. Every answer is assembled from primary law and returned with its citations intact — so verification is a matter of following a link, not repeating the research.
